> **"Complexity is the enemy of execution."**

WorkflowWizerd is a minimalist productivity tool built to eliminate decision fatigue. Instead of overwhelming you with a "wall of tasks," it transforms complex projects into a guided, step-by-step experience.

## ‚ú® The Philosophy
Most project management tools act as digital filing cabinets‚Äîthey store information but don't help you execute it. WorkflowWizerd acts as a **GPS for your work**. 

By utilizing a "Wizard" interface, the app focuses your attention on the **one next thing** you need to do, hiding the mental noise of the entire project until you are ready for it.

## üöÄ Key Features
- **Sequential Task Logic:** Tasks are revealed one by one. You only see Step B when Step A is complete.
- **Progress Tracking:** A dynamic, visual progress bar that updates in real-time.
- **Distraction-Free UI:** A minimalist dashboard designed to reduce cognitive load and keep you in "Flow State."
- **Rule-Based Automation:** Built-in logic that manages task dependencies and project states.
